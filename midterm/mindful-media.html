Do you every catch yourself mindlessly scrolling through TikTok, Instagram Reels, or other social media platforms? With its constant presence in our lives, reaching for our phones to chase that fleeting dopamine rush has become second nature. Instead of letting social media be a mindless habit or falling into the doom scroll tunnel, we can use media in a productive way. The right spaces can offer endless opportunities to learn and engage meaningfully. Here are some alternative options to break away from the endless scroll:

  • Profound Pondering: Spiritual wisdom & life advice
  • Eternalised: Psychology, meaning, & self-reflection
  • Pursuit of Wonder: Philosophy, science, literature, well-being

    • By shifting your feed to more intentional, enriching content, you can still enjoy social media while avoiding the doom tunnel
